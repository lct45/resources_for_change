we're glad you're here
we created this project to help curate resources regarding what we can all do during this time, as so many great things have been shared on a wide variety of platforms. In this document, you can find resources on how to educate ourselves, take action, donate, support black businesses, and resources others have created. The most important part of this page is that THE WORK IS NOT DONE. We are relying on you all to share this with folks and ADD TO THIS LIST. You can find a tab on the link to submit resources and content that you have been seeing and consuming recently that you feel is valuable to share with others.
